Exhibition of vintage cars

Exhibition Régis Mathieu

The first Provencal exhibition combining art and vintage cars

In Gargas, in the Vaucluse, from December 16, 2022 to January 22, 2023, the chandelier Mathieu held an exhibition dedicated to the Porsche 911. The exhibition is open from 17h to 21h every day until January 22.

Date and place

It is a luminous meeting between a crazy creator of chandeliers and a seasoned Porsche collector. 40 cars as rare as historical are gathered there alongside chandeliers. This collector is named Régis Mathieu, founder of the chandelier factory. Each car was positioned above a chandelier.

The flagship models

Porsche 356 Speedster (1948-1965)

The 356 Speedster model is for many the most beautiful of the German brand. It has an air-cooled 4-cylinder engine (boxer), with a power that goes up to 130 horsepower.

911 « Classic » (1964)

Much sought after by collectors, this model is the reference of the brand. It has a 2-liter flat-six engine (always placed at the rear) that is air-cooled. It has a power of 130 horsepower and can go up to 210 km/h roaring with its beautiful voice, very appreciated.

911 Turbo S Sonauto

Sonauto (former Porsche France importer) is behind this seduction operation. This first Turbo S in the history of the brand, receives as it should be an engine preparation, which makes it gain 30 hp. Externally, it benefits from a unique color, a specific front spoiler, 4 exhaust outlets and a lowered chassis, the rear logo has disappeared.
